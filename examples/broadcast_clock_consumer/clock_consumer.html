About this exerciser

Receives every clock event defined by BroadcastTimeS Apr 25, 2021 and lets you send the consumer-side commands defined in spec Section 4.5-4.10. Pair it with the broadcast_clock_source example (or any other generator on the bus) and watch the readout track that producer.

Local Interpolation
A clock display should keep its own internal clock to extrapolate fast-time between Reports — BroadcastTimeTN Section 2.6.2. start(clockId) tells the library to advance time locally; stop() freezes it until the next Report.
